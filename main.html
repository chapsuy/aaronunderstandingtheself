The Myth of Icarus

The illustration I drew is of a human from a famous myth called "The Myth of Icarus". It's about a man whose ambition led him to push his limits too far, ultimately causing his demise. The story of Icarus resonates with me deeply.

When I was working at home, I had to do all the chores by myself. I pushed myself to finish everything as fast as I could without taking a break. Eventually, I got sick and experienced difficulty breathing for an entire month. Every day felt like torture, even though I was taking medication. During that time, I realized that if I hadn't pushed myself too hard, I could have avoided getting sick and would have been able to do more than just lie in bed.

This experience taught me that I shouldn't push myself beyond my limits to achieve something that isn't more precious than my health. Now, I understand the importance of knowing my limits and taking breaks when needed and need to listen to my body as well.
